① Load your stuff  ·  ② Quiz yourself  ·  ③ Refresh & it's gone

Load Your Data

Upload .txt or .csv files. One entry per line:

term | definition | notes (optional)
hello | hola | greeting
goodbye | adiós | farewell

Embed media by placing a URL in the term or definition field:

Image (.jpg .png .gif .webp .svg)
https://example.com/photo.jpg | What is this? | notes

Video (.mp4 .webm .ogg)
https://example.com/clip.mp4 | Name this scene | notes

Audio (.mp3 .wav .ogg)
https://example.com/sound.mp3 | What language is this? | notes

YouTube
https://youtube.com/watch?v=VIDEO_ID | Video title | notes

Text before or after the URL becomes a label above the media.

To use Fetch Specialties with your own repo, structure it like this:

your-repo/
  vocabulary/
    Spanish/
      basics.txt
      verbs.txt
    Science/
      cells.txt
    Any Specialty/
      any_file.txt

Subdirectory names become specialty names. Files inside become loadable decks.
